Mentor Weekly Check-In Resources

Week Pillar Focus Conversation Pointers Question Prompts Action Step
Week 1 Personal Development Building Self-Confidence Normalize nerves. Highlight strengths. What made you proud this week? When did you doubt yourself? Speak up once in a group or class.
Week 2 Entrepreneurship Idea Brainstorming Encourage creativity. Avoid judgment. What problem do you want to solve? Why does it matter? Write your idea in one sentence.
Week 3 Academic Study Skills Focus on habits, not grades. How do you study now? What distractions get in the way? Try one new study habit.
