WeekVirtual Activity (Small & Quick)Benchmark Points to Hit
1The Wins Folder: Create a desktop folder/Notes page. List 3 wins.Identify 3 core strengths.
2"The 1% Rule": In chat, identify one <2 min habit for easier life.Commit to 1 daily action.
3Feedback Flip: Mentor gives "tough" critique. Mentee types professional reply.Use critique to improve task.
4Social Audit: Screen share profile. Identify 3 things to "clean up."Understand Digital First Impressions.
5The Reflection GIF: Send a GIF representing mood/growth. Explain why.Habit of self-evaluation.
6The "Why" Drill: Mentor asks "Why?" 3 times about a specific goal.Connect values to goals.
7Digital Calendar: Screen share to set up a "Deep Work" block.Prioritization using tools.
8Collaborative Doc: Both edit a doc to solve a fake crisis.Demonstrate real-time teamwork.
9SMART Note: Type one specific, measurable goal with a deadline.Draft a realistic 30-day plan.
10The Pivot Story: Share a "fail" story and identify the pivot point.Define resilience in own words.
11The Pro/Con Table: Use a table in chat to weigh a real decision.Use logic over emotion.
12"Who Am I Now?": 2-min video call speech on growth since Week 1.Articulate growth journey.
WeekVirtual Activity (Small & Quick)Benchmark Points to Hit
1"Annoyance Mine": List 5 things that "suck" in the neighborhood. Pick 1.Define a problem worth solving.

9Mission Tweet: Write business mission in <280 characters.Define "Purpose" clearly.
10Content Draft: Create a "hook" for a TikTok/IG business post.Apply marketing psychology.
11Relationship Map: List 5 businesses to partner with for promotion.Identify Power Partnerships.
12The Final Deck: Share 3-slide presentation (Problem/Solution).Present a completed business case.
