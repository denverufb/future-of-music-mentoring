WeekVirtual Activity (Small & Quick)Benchmark Points to Hit
1The Wins Folder: Create a folder on desktop or Notes. List 3 wins.Identify 3 core strengths.
2"The 1% Rule": In chat, identify one small habit (<2 min) that makes life easier.Commit to 1 daily independent action.
3Feedback Flip: Mentor gives "tough" critique. Mentee types professional reply.Use critique to improve a specific task.
4Social Audit: Screen share profile. Identify 3 things to "clean up" or add.Understand "Digital First Impressions."
5The Reflection GIF: Send a GIF/Meme representing mood/growth. Explain why.Habit of self-evaluation.
6The "Why" Drill: Mentee states goal; mentor asks "Why?" 3 times.Connect personal values to goals.
7Digital Calendar Setup: Screen share to set up a "Deep Work" block.Use a digital tool for prioritization.
8Collaborative Doc: Both edit a Doc simultaneously to solve a fake crisis.Demonstrate real-time teamwork.
9SMART Note: Type one specific, measurable goal with a deadline.Draft a realistic 30-day plan.
10The Pivot Story: Share story of a "fail" and identify pivot point.Define resilience in own words.
11The Pro/Con Table: Use a table in chat to weigh a real decision.Use logic over emotion for a choice.
12"Who Am I Now?": 2-minute video call speech on how they've changed.Articulate personal growth journey.
WeekVirtual Activity (Small & Quick)Benchmark Points to Hit
1The "Annoyance Mine": List 5 things that "suck" in neighborhood. Pick 1.Define a problem worth solving.
2Google Trends Check: Screen share Trends to see interest in idea/topic.Use data to prove market interest.
3The Startup Spreadsheet: 3-column list: Item, Cost, Priority (Need/Want).Calculate "Minimum Viable" costs.
4Canva Logo Sprint: Use Canva to pick font and color palette in 10 mins.Create a basic visual brand identity.
5The "Angry Email" Fix: Mentor pastes fake bad review. Mentee drafts reply.Demonstrate "Customer First" empathy.
6Voice Memo Pitch: Mentee records 30-sec pitch and sends to mentor.Deliver a clear, confident pitch.
7Competitor DM: Find similar biz on IG/TikTok. List 3 pros and 3 cons.Identify a "Competitive Advantage."
8The "Bag" Calculator: Calculate: Price - Costs = Profit for 10, 50, 100 sales.Understand basic unit economics.
9Mission Tweet: Write a business Mission Statement (<280 chars).Define "Purpose" clearly.
10Content Draft: Create one "hook" for a TikTok or IG business post.Apply marketing psychology.
11Relationship Map: List 5 partners for cross-promotion.Identify "Power Partnerships."
12The Final Deck: Share 3-slide "Final Presentation" (Problem, Solution, Future).Present a completed business case.
WeekVirtual Activity (Small & Quick)Benchmark Points to Hit
1Folder Cleanup: Screen share. Organize files into subject folders.Create a digital organization system.
2The Syllabus Highlight: Highlight assignments >20% of grade for month.Focus effort on high-impact tasks.
3Podcast Summary: Listen to 5-min educational clip. Type 3 bullets.Practice comprehension without text.
4Scanning Race: Find specific answer in online article in <30 seconds.Improve digital reading speed.
5The "Clickbait" Test: Mentor shares headline; find source to verify.Analyze source credibility.
6The "Blurting" Note: Timer for 1 min. Type everything remembered from class.Practice active recall (retention).
7Exam Countdown: Mark Exam Date in red. Work backward to set 3 dates.Plan for long-term prep.
8AI Fact-Checker: Use AI to explain concept; find 1 error/over-simplification.Master efficient research.
9The Outline "Skeleton": Type 3 Headings for a hypothetical essay.Structure thoughts before writing.
10Presentation Practice: Share screen and talk through 3 slides.Speak confidently without notes.
11Group Work Script: Draft professional message to "lazy" group member.Practice group leadership.
12The Grade Reflection: Compare current habits to Week 1. Log what worked.Self-assess academic growth.

To keep engagement high, encourage mentors to award "Street Credits" (SC):

  • Attendance: 10 SC (Showing up on time)
  • Engagement: 10 SC (Active participation in conversation)
  • Challenge Mastery: 20 SC (Completing weekly virtual activity)
  • Bonus "Street Tax" Point: 5 SC (When a mentee pushes through a difficult critique)
