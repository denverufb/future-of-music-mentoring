Welcome to the Future of Music Mentoring Program

A free, youth-friendly mentoring program supporting young musicians and creatives. Set weekly goals, check in each week, and get feedback from your mentors.

Submit Weekly Goals Weekly Check-In

Forms are hosted externally (Google Forms or Formspree) and embedded on the following pages. If you can't see an embedded form, open it in a new tab using the link on the form page.

How it works

  1. Each week, submit one or more goals through the "Submit Weekly Goals" form.
  2. During the week (or at week's end), complete the "Weekly Check-In" to record progress.
  3. Admins will review submissions, leave feedback, and track mentee progress over time.
